Friends of the Museum

About

Friends of the Museum are helpers and supporters of Oundle Museum some of whom are also Stewards and Volunteers. They are the backbone of support and actively assist with the annual events to making regular donations that go towards developing the museum and its exhibitions.

Become a Friend

You may become a Friend of The Museum by making a small donation on an annual basis, by completing the form below. ‘Friends of the Museum’ get invited to the Official Museum opening every year at the beginning of March, receive quarterly newsletters and invitations to the Museum Events.

Volunteering

If you have time or expertise to offer then why not become a volunteer or Museum Steward? Oundle Museum is an independent, self-funded charity (Registered Charity No. 1202160). It is run by the people of Oundle for the community and visitors, for young and old alike.
